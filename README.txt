JESS LIFE & CEO PLANNER — WORKFLOW & FINANCE UPDATE

HOW TO OPEN
1. Unzip the folder.
2. Double-click index.html.
3. It opens in Chrome, Safari, Edge, or another modern browser.

COST
There is no subscription, hosting, login, or Replit required. The planner runs locally on your device.

SAVING
Your planner saves automatically in the browser and device you use.
Data saved in Chrome will not automatically appear in Safari or on another computer.
Use Backup & Settings regularly so your information is protected.

TASK WORKFLOW
- Daily tasks can be checked off.
- Progress updates automatically for each day and the current week.
- Each task can be moved to tomorrow or to any chosen date.
- A single button moves all unfinished tasks to tomorrow.
- Unfinished tasks from earlier days appear in a review area and can be carried forward.
- Completed tasks remain on the day they were completed.
- The Weekly Planner shows each day’s checklist and progress.
- Master tasks can be scheduled for today, tomorrow, or another date.
- Scheduled master tasks appear in the Daily and Weekly planners.

FINANCE CENTRE
The Finance Centre now follows the same structure as the R2R finance workbook:
- Client or vendor
- Description
- Expected or due date
- Received or paid date
- Category
- Amount
- HST included
- Status
- Payment method
- Notes

It also includes:
- Current bank balance and bank-balance history
- Projected 30-day balance
- Expected and actual income and expenses
- Automatic overdue income and overdue bill totals
- Bills due in the next seven days
- Next five bills
- Projected month-end balance
- Weekly, monthly, Summer, Winter, and operating-year views
- Recurring weekly, bi-weekly, twice-monthly, monthly, quarterly, and yearly cash flow
- Summer view from May through October
- Winter view from November through April
- A twelve-month operating-year table from May through April

RECURRING PAYMENTS
Bi-weekly means every 14 days.
Twice monthly is used when the same amount comes out twice each month.
When two monthly payments have different amounts, enter them as two separate monthly recurring items.

Use Record Paid or Record Received when the next recurring occurrence happens. The planner records the actual transaction and advances the next date.

BACKUPS AND UPDATING
This version uses the same saved-data key as the earlier planner, so existing information in the same browser should remain available.
Before replacing an older version, download a backup from Backup & Settings.
The backup contains task history, carryovers, planner entries, bank history, finances, goals, habits, and notes.

IMPORTANT
Do not clear browser site data without downloading a backup first.
Private or incognito mode is not recommended because saved data may be erased.
